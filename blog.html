The Basilisks NFT

Tony Nguyen | 20 Jun 2022

Basilisks aren't just creatures - they're digital apex predators. Forged on the Ethereum chain and raised in a metaverse colosseum, they live to dominate,duel,and devour. Every one of them is an NFT with unique DNA, traits, and bloodlust.

  • In this game, your Basilisk is your worrior. You breed it. You name it. You train it. And when you're ready, you send it in to the arena - not to play but to survive.
  • Each NFT Basilisk has a set of traits, mutation and skills, minted immutable on-chain. The arena? A fully 3D metaverse battleground with wagers, livestreams, and faction wars.Players don't just watch - they stake tokens, call hits, and climb leaderboards.
  • The games AI learns from every move. If your Basilisk gets too strong - it becomes a target. If it dies? it's burned forever. If it's wins? you earn crypto,glory and power.
  • Unique DNA structure
  • PvP-enable arena
  • Permanent death mode
  • Metaverse integration
